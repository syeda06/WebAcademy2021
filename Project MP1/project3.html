October 25, 2021

Syeda Samantha

This third movie of mine is "Raees", directed by Rahul Dholakia. This movie is about a kid who works for people that sell illegal alcohol, who eventually grows up and tries to create his own alcohol-selling business in hopes of creating a city or town for the good of his people. He grows up to overthrow the businesses of his former bosses. This movie also has a sad ending.
